支付问题 PAYMENT
Q: 支持哪些支付方式？| What payment methods are supported?
A: 我们支持 PayPal 支付，包括 Visa、Mastercard、American Express 以及各国本地 PayPal 支付方式，覆盖全球 200+ 国家和地区。
A: We support PayPal payments, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and local PayPal payment methods, covering 200+ countries and regions worldwide.
Q: 支付后套餐多久生效？| How long does it take for the package to activate after payment?
A: PayPal 支付成功后，套餐权益将在 5-10 秒内自动解锁，无需手动操作。如遇延迟，请刷新页面或联系客服。
A: After successful PayPal payment, your package will be activated within 5-10 seconds automatically. If there is a delay, please refresh the page or contact support.
Q: 支付失败了怎么办？| What if my payment fails?
A: 请检查 PayPal 账户余额或银行卡状态，确保网络连接稳定。如问题持续，请稍后重试或联系 support@mychinesename.co。
A: Please check your PayPal account balance or bank card status and ensure your network connection is stable. If the problem persists, please try again later or contact support@mychinesename.co.
Q: 可以退款吗？| Can I get a refund?
A: 由于数字内容服务的特殊性，支付成功后不支持主动退款。如有支付异常（重复扣款、未到账等），请在 72 小时内联系客服。
A: Due to the nature of digital content services, all payments are non-refundable once processed. For payment anomalies (duplicate charges, non-receipt, etc.), please contact support within 72 hours.
起名规则 NAMING
Q: 起名需要提供哪些信息？| What information do I need to provide for naming?
A: 必填：英文名（Given Name）和姓氏（Surname）；选填：性别、出生年月日时、偏好风格、期望寓意。这些信息帮助我们生成更精准的姓名。
A: Required: Given Name and Surname; Optional: Gender, Birth Date & Time, Style Preference, Meaning Preference. This information helps us generate more accurate names.
Q: 生成的姓名可以商用吗？| Can the generated names be used commercially?
A: 付费套餐姓名可用于个人非商业用途，如社交媒体昵称、个人品牌。商用（如商业代言、产品命名）请联系我们获取授权。
A: Paid-package names can be used for personal non-commercial purposes such as social media nicknames and personal branding. For commercial use (e.g. commercial endorsement, product naming), please contact us for authorization.
Q: 不满意生成的结果可以重新生成吗？| Can I regenerate if I am not satisfied with the result?
A: 基础版有 1 次免费重生成机会；Premium 和 Ultimate 套餐不限次数重生成。直接点击「重新生成」按钮即可。
A: Basic plan has 1 free regeneration; Premium and Ultimate plans have unlimited regenerations. Simply click the Regenerate button.
Q: 五行分析是什么？| What is Five-Element (Wuxing) analysis?
A: 根据您的出生时间，结合八字命理分析五行喜用，帮助选择与您命格相合的汉字。所有套餐均包含基础五行解析，Premium/Ultimate 提供完整分析。
A: Based on your birth time, we analyze the Five Elements (Wuxing) to identify favorable elements, helping select characters that match your destiny. All plans include basic analysis; Premium/Ultimate provide full analysis.
Q: 典故溯源是什么意思？| What is "Cultural Source Tracing"?
A: Premium 和 Ultimate 套餐提供生成姓名的古籍出处，解释名字中每个字在《诗经》《楚辞》《论语》《易经》等典籍中的来源和寓意。
A: Premium and Ultimate plans provide the classical source of each character in the generated name, explaining its origin and meaning in classics such as Book of Songs, Chu Ci, Analects, and I Ching.
